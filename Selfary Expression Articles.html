Articles focus on readers' ability to choose different points of view regarding their thoughts, feelings, and beliefs. After taking in information, the timing of fully understanding that information can happen at any point of our lives - weeks, months, or years in the future. By "fully", I view that as all the different points of view regarding that topic, not only one side or part of it. (if you disagree with something, that is a big indication that you are viewing something partially) The information can also apply to any point of our lives - past, present, or future. Keeping an open mind is the only way to see the different points of view of the information that has been read, listened to, and/or experienced.
