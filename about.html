I'm Tempest

I created Selfary to be a non-profit caring about changing the robotic hiring practices online that have caused the personal touch of hiring to be lost because everything is automated and behind computer screens.

I have years of experience in Human Resources, working with HRIS implementation, data analysis, administration, training, and onboarding/recruiting. I started with Human Resources when I was 19 years old and continue to do this work outside of this non-profit as well.

I also have experience with understanding how hiring processes within businesses – big and small – are affecting everyday people because of the carelessness of people’s time and experiences. This then causes carelessness on the employee’s end because of businesses carelessness to begin with. And the cycle repeats.

This is not sustainable ethically. Humanity will be lost due to carelessness, which turns into purposeless and worthlessness, unless a shift starts happening. Big time.

My purpose with Selfary is to lead people to work towards shifting our online hiring practices from carelessness to care. With that in mind, I created this business as a non-profit because of the unethical aspects of non-profits themselves too. To read articles that are about experiences and ethics regarding hiring practices, including business expenditures, feel free to view the Hiring Ethics articles.
