Employer Search By Region

Welcome to Selfary - An Employer Search Database — a simple, organized way to explore business and government options across the United States.

These filters can help you quickly browse employers by location, business and government scale, and type of employment, i.e., permanent or temporary employers. The purpose is to provide a comprehensive list of employers for job searches, general data research, or having a clear point of view with how our online hiring world is structured.

A large reason for this employer search is to lessen the likelihood of spam, ads, and scammers that people go through during their hiring endeavors - to be able to strive towards our purposeful goals with ease. While Selfary does not have control over what is happening behind closed doors with our businesses and governments, Selfary can provide information to be readily available for all of us to improve the online "system" we live in through creating caring and geninue experiences instead of disconnected and careless ones.
